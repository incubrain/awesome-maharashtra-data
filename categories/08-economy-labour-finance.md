# Economy, Labour & Finance

> Economic indicators, employment surveys, financial data, and budget datasets for Maharashtra economic analysis and fintech AI.

**Priority**: Medium | **Target**: 10+ entries

| Dataset | Size | License | Modality | Links | AI Use Case |
|---------|------|---------|----------|-------|-------------|
| **RBI DBIE (Database on Indian Economy)** — Comprehensive economic and financial statistics covering real sector, financial markets, external sector, and public finance with Maharashtra state-level data where available. | Multi-year time series | Open Government | Tabular (Excel, CSV, PDF) | [DBIE Portal](https://data.rbi.org.in/DBIE/) &#124; [RBI Statistics](https://www.rbi.org.in/Scripts/Statistics.aspx) | Macroeconomic forecasting, financial trend analysis, state-level economic modelling |
| **PLFS (Periodic Labour Force Survey) — Maharashtra** — Quarterly and annual employment and unemployment indicators including LFPR, WPR, and unemployment rates with state-level Maharashtra tabulations. | ~1 lakh+ households nationally, quarterly | Open (microdata access) | Tabular (unit-level data, reports) | [PLFS Microdata](https://microdata.gov.in/NADA/index.php/catalog/PLFS) &#124; [MoSPI Reports](https://mospi.gov.in/download-reports?main_cat=ODU5&cat=All&sub_category=All) | Labour market analytics, unemployment prediction, informal sector modelling |
| **CMIE Consumer Pyramids — Maharashtra** — Longitudinal panel survey of household income, expenditure, employment, and consumer behaviour across Maharashtra (requires subscription). | ~1.7 lakh households nationally, ongoing | Proprietary (subscription) | Tabular (API, dashboard) | [CMIE](https://consumerpyramidsdx.cmie.com/) | Consumer behaviour modelling, household income prediction, economic impact assessment |
| **Maharashtra Economic Survey** — Annual publication by the Directorate of Economics and Statistics covering GSDP, agriculture, industry, infrastructure, and social indicators for Maharashtra. | State-level, annual | Open Government | Tabular (PDF) | [Finance Dept](https://finance.maharashtra.gov.in/en/publication/economic-survey-of-maharashtra-2023-24/) &#124; [DES Maharashtra](https://mahades.maharashtra.gov.in/esm.do?type=R) &#124; [MAITRI](https://maitri.maharashtra.gov.in/resources/economic-survey-of-maharashtra/) | State economic planning, sector-wise growth analysis, policy impact evaluation |
| **MSME Udyam Registration — Maharashtra** — Enterprise-level registration data for micro, small, and medium enterprises in Maharashtra including district-wise distribution and sector classification. | 8.9 lakh+ MSMEs in Maharashtra | Open Government | Tabular (CSV, dashboard) | [data.gov.in](https://www.data.gov.in/resource/list-msme-registered-units-under-udyam) &#124; [MSME Dashboard](https://dashboard.msme.gov.in/Udyam_Statewise.aspx) | MSME cluster analysis, entrepreneurship ecosystem modelling, economic development planning |
| **NSO Annual Survey of Industries (ASI) — Maharashtra** — Factory-level data on manufacturing output, employment, capital investment, and value added from registered factories in Maharashtra. | Factory-level, annual | Open (microdata with registration) | Tabular (unit-level data) | [MoSPI ASI](https://www.mospi.gov.in/annual-survey-industries) &#124; [eSankhyiki](https://esankhyiki.mospi.gov.in/) | Industrial productivity analysis, manufacturing sector modelling, employment trend forecasting |
| **Maharashtra Budget Documents** — Detailed state budget data including revenue receipts, expenditure by department, plan/non-plan breakdowns, and fiscal indicators. | State-level, annual | Open Government | Tabular (PDF, Excel) | [Maharashtra Budget](https://beams.mahakosh.gov.in/) &#124; [PRS Analysis](https://prsindia.org/files/budget/budget_state/maharashtra/2024/Maharashtra_Budget_Analysis_2024-25.pdf) | Fiscal analysis, budget allocation optimisation, public expenditure modelling |
| **India Innovation Index — Maharashtra** — Composite innovation performance ranking covering human capital, knowledge output, business sophistication, and research for Maharashtra. | State-level, annual | Open Government | Tabular (PDF, web) | [NITI Aayog Innovation](https://www.niti.gov.in/india-innovation-index) | Innovation ecosystem benchmarking, R&D policy analysis, state competitiveness modelling |
| **Maharashtra Socio-Economic Review** — Annual statistical publication covering population, agriculture, industry, infrastructure, and welfare indicators from the DES Maharashtra. | State & district level, annual | Open Government | Tabular (PDF) | [DES Maharashtra](https://mahades.maharashtra.gov.in/) | Socio-economic trend analysis, district-level development modelling |
| **GST Revenue Data — Maharashtra** — Monthly Goods and Services Tax collection data for Maharashtra, the largest contributor to national GST revenue. | Monthly, state-level | Open Government | Tabular (press releases, PDF) | [GST Council](https://gstcouncil.gov.in/) &#124; [PIB Releases](https://pib.gov.in/) | Tax revenue forecasting, economic activity proxy modelling, compliance analytics |
