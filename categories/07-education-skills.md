# Education & Skills

> School statistics, higher education data, skill development, and literacy datasets for Maharashtra education AI and workforce planning.

**Priority**: Medium | **Target**: 8+ entries

| Dataset | Size | License | Modality | Links | AI Use Case |
|---------|------|---------|----------|-------|-------------|
| **UDISE+ (Unified District Information System for Education)** — Comprehensive annual school-level data on enrolment, infrastructure, teachers, and facilities for all recognised schools in Maharashtra. | ~1.1 lakh+ schools in Maharashtra | Open Government | Tabular (web portal, reports) | [UDISE+ Portal](https://udiseplus.gov.in/) &#124; [Dashboard](https://dashboard.udiseplus.gov.in/) &#124; [Reports](https://udiseplus.gov.in/udisereport/) | Education access modelling, school infrastructure gap analysis, dropout prediction |
| **ASER (Annual Status of Education Report) — Maharashtra** — Household-based survey of children's enrolment status and learning outcomes (reading, arithmetic) across rural Maharashtra districts. | District-level, annual since 2005 | Open (with attribution) | Tabular (PDF, Excel) | [ASER Centre](https://asercentre.org/) &#124; [Maharashtra PDF](https://asercentre.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/12/Maharashtra-1.pdf) | Learning outcome prediction, education quality assessment, intervention targeting |
| **NIRF Rankings — Maharashtra Institutions** — National Institutional Ranking Framework data covering teaching, research, perception, and outcome metrics for Maharashtra higher education institutions. | ~1,000+ institutions annually | Open Government | Tabular (PDF, web) | [NIRF Portal](https://www.nirfindia.org/) &#124; [Kaggle Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/iitanshravan/nirf-rankings-dataset-20162025) | Institutional benchmarking, higher education quality analytics |
| **AISHE (All India Survey on Higher Education) — Maharashtra** — Annual census of higher education institutions covering student enrolment, faculty, programmes, examination results, and finances for Maharashtra. | State-level, 42,825 colleges nationally | Open Government | Tabular (PDF, web) | [AISHE Portal](https://aishe.gov.in/) &#124; [Final Reports](https://aishe.gov.in/aishe-final-report/) &#124; [DHE Pune](https://dhepune.gov.in/aishe-2/) | Higher education access analytics, faculty-student ratio optimisation, enrolment forecasting |
| **Skill India / NSDC Dashboard — Maharashtra** — Skill development training data including candidates trained, certified, and placed across various sectors in Maharashtra via NSDC programmes. | State-level, ongoing | Open Government | Tabular (dashboard) | [NSDC Maharashtra](https://nsdcindia.org/maharashtra) &#124; [SkillSIP Dashboard](https://skillsip.nsdcindia.org/dashboard) | Skill gap identification, workforce planning, training programme effectiveness analysis |
| **National Achievement Survey (NAS) — Maharashtra** — Large-scale assessment of student learning outcomes in Classes 3, 5, 8, and 10 across Maharashtra government and private schools. | ~34 lakh students nationally (2021) | Open Government | Tabular (PDF, dashboard) | [NAS Portal](https://nas.gov.in/) &#124; [NCERT State Reports](https://www.ncert.nic.in/src.php?ln=) &#124; [PARAKH Dashboard](https://parakh.ncert.gov.in/nas-dashboard) | Student performance prediction, learning gap analysis, education policy evaluation |
| **NSS 75th Round — Education Survey** — Household Social Consumption: Education survey covering participation, expenditure, and out-of-school indicators with Maharashtra state-level tabulations. | 1,13,757 households nationally | Open (microdata access) | Tabular (unit-level data) | [Microdata Portal](https://microdata.gov.in/NADA/index.php/catalog/151) &#124; [MoSPI](https://www.mospi.gov.in/unit-level-data-report-nss-75th-round-schedule-252july-2017-june-2018social-consumption-education) | Education expenditure modelling, out-of-school children identification, equity analysis |
| **Maharashtra State Board Exam Results** — Annual SSC (Class 10) and HSC (Class 12) examination results data from the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary and Higher Secondary Education. | Millions of students annually | Open Government | Tabular (web portal) | [MSBSHSE](https://www.mahahsscboard.in/) | Student performance analytics, pass rate prediction, subject-wise trend analysis |
