# Health & Nutrition

> Public health, disease surveillance, nutrition, and healthcare facility datasets for Maharashtra health AI and public service delivery.

**Priority**: Medium | **Target**: 10+ entries

| Dataset | Size | License | Modality | Links | AI Use Case |
|---------|------|---------|----------|-------|-------------|
| **NFHS-5 Maharashtra** — National Family Health Survey (2019-21) district-level data on health, nutrition, fertility, and demographic indicators for Maharashtra. | ~36 districts, 31,643 households surveyed | Open / DHS | Tabular (CSV, PDF) | [IIPS/NFHS](http://rchiips.org/nfhs/) &#124; [GitHub CSV](https://github.com/pratapvardhan/NFHS-5) &#124; [DHS Report](https://dhsprogram.com/pubs/pdf/FR374/FR374_Maharashtra.pdf) | Public health analytics, nutrition gap identification, maternal/child health modelling |
| **HMIS (Health Management Information System)** — Monthly facility-level health data from all government health facilities across Maharashtra covering maternal health, immunisation, disease reporting. | All districts, monthly from 2008 | Open Government | Tabular (web portal) | [HMIS Portal](https://hmis.mohfw.gov.in/) &#124; [IHME GHDx](https://ghdx.healthdata.org/series/india-health-management-information-system-hmis) | Health facility performance monitoring, disease trend analysis, resource allocation models |
| **IDSP (Integrated Disease Surveillance Programme)** — Weekly disease outbreak data covering epidemic-prone diseases across Maharashtra districts in syndromic, probable, and lab-confirmed formats. | Weekly reports, 720+ districts nationally | Open Government | Tabular (web reports) | [IDSP Portal](https://idsp.mohfw.gov.in/) &#124; [NCDC](https://ncdc.mohfw.gov.in/integrated-disease-surveillance-programme/) | Epidemic early-warning systems, disease outbreak prediction, syndromic surveillance AI |
| **National Health Profile (NHP) — Maharashtra** — Comprehensive health statistics including infrastructure, human resources, disease burden, and vital statistics for Maharashtra. | State-level, annual | Open Government | Tabular (PDF, Excel) | [CBHI NHP](https://cbhidghs.mohfw.gov.in/) | Healthcare planning models, health infrastructure gap analysis |
| **CPCB Air Quality — Maharashtra Stations** — Continuous ambient air quality monitoring data (PM2.5, PM10, NO2, SO2, O3) from CAAQMS stations across Maharashtra cities. | Hourly, 20+ Maharashtra stations | Open Government | Tabular (CSV, API) | [CPCB Real-time](https://cpcb.nic.in/real-time-air-qulity-data/) &#124; [CCR Portal](https://airquality.cpcb.gov.in/ccr/) | Air quality health impact modelling, pollution-health correlation, AQI forecasting |
| **ICMR National Cancer Registry — Maharashtra** — Population-based cancer registry data from multiple Maharashtra registries (Mumbai, Pune, Aurangabad, Nagpur, Barshi rural). | Multi-year, 7+ registries | Open (with acknowledgement) | Tabular (PDF, web) | [NCDIR Reports](https://ncdirindia.org/Reports.aspx) &#124; [Maharashtra Factsheet](https://ncdirindia.org/All_Reports/State_Factsheet_21/Factsheet/FS_Maharashtra.pdf) | Cancer incidence prediction, epidemiological modelling, screening programme optimisation |
| **Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY / MJPJAY Claims Data** — Health insurance claims data from the Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana and Maharashtra's Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana. | 3.57 Cr+ claims nationally | Restricted (portal access) | Tabular (dashboard) | [NHA PM-JAY](https://nha.gov.in/PM-JAY) &#124; [MJPJAY](https://www.jeevandayee.gov.in/) | Health expenditure analysis, claims fraud detection, hospital performance analytics |
| **NITI Aayog Health Index** — Composite state health performance rankings across health outcomes, governance, and key inputs/processes for Maharashtra. | State-level, annual rounds | Open Government | Tabular (PDF, Excel) | [Health Index Dashboard](https://social.niti.gov.in/hlt-ranking) &#124; [Reports](https://social.niti.gov.in/health-index) | State health benchmarking, policy impact evaluation, health governance analytics |
| **IHME Global Burden of Disease — India Subnational** — Comprehensive disease burden estimates (292 causes, 88 risk factors) for Maharashtra from 1990 to present via the India State-Level Disease Burden Initiative. | Subnational, 1990–2023 | Open (IHME Free-of-Charge) | Tabular (CSV, interactive) | [GBD Results Tool](https://vizhub.healthdata.org/gbd-results/) &#124; [GHDx](https://ghdx.healthdata.org/gbd-2021) | Disease burden forecasting, risk factor modelling, health intervention prioritisation |
| **WHO Global Health Observatory — India** — Disaggregated health indicators for India including mortality, morbidity, health systems, and risk factors from the WHO data repository. | Country-level, multi-year | Open (CC BY-NC-SA 3.0 IGO) | Tabular (CSV, API) | [GHO Portal](https://www.who.int/data/gho/) | Cross-country health benchmarking, SDG health indicator tracking |
| **Maharashtra Public Health Department Annual Reports** — Official annual health statistics from the Directorate of Health Services covering government hospital performance, disease incidence, and programme outcomes. | State-level, annual | Open Government | Tabular (PDF) | [DHS Maharashtra](https://arogya.maharashtra.gov.in/) | Health programme evaluation, service delivery gap analysis |
