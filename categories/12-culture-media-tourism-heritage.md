# Culture, Media, Tourism & Heritage

> Public domain Marathi literature, heritage site data, tourism statistics, and cultural media datasets for Maharashtra cultural AI and digital humanities.

**Priority**: Medium | **Target**: 8+ entries

| Dataset | Size | License | Modality | Links | AI Use Case |
|---------|------|---------|----------|-------|-------------|
| **Wikisource Marathi** — Public domain Marathi literature including 1,000+ books from Maharashtra Granthottejak Sanstha, classical texts (Dnyaneshwari, Dasbodh, Haripath), and historical documents. | 1,000+ books, growing | CC BY-SA 4.0 | Text (Marathi) | [mr.wikisource.org](https://mr.wikisource.org/) &#124; [CLTK GitHub](https://github.com/cltk/marathi_text_wikisource) | Literary text mining, historical language modelling, OCR training for Marathi scripts |
| **ASI Protected Monuments — Maharashtra** — List of 285 centrally protected and 244 state protected archaeological monuments and heritage sites in Maharashtra maintained by the Archaeological Survey of India. | 529 monuments | Open Government | Tabular (PDF, web) | [ASI Maharashtra](https://asi.nic.in/protected-monuments-in-maharashtra/) &#124; [CPM List](https://asi.nic.in/pdf/CPM_List.pdf) &#124; [Indian Heritage](https://www.indianheritage.gov.in/showgallery) | Heritage site cataloguing, tourism route optimisation, cultural preservation analytics |
| **UNESCO World Heritage Sites — Maharashtra** — Detailed documentation for Maharashtra's UNESCO World Heritage Sites including Ajanta Caves, Ellora Caves, Elephanta Caves, Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus, and Victorian Gothic/Art Deco ensembles of Mumbai. | 5 sites | Open | Text, Images (web) | [UNESCO WHC](https://whc.unesco.org/) | Heritage tourism analytics, cultural significance analysis, visitor flow modelling |
| **National Digital Library of India (NDLI) — Marathi Collection** — Digital library providing access to Marathi books, manuscripts, lecture videos, and research articles across multiple disciplines, with Marathi language interface. | 81M+ items nationally, Marathi subset | Open | Text, Multimedia | [NDLI Portal](https://ndl.iitkgp.ac.in/) &#124; [NDLI Search](https://www.ndl.gov.in/) | Marathi knowledge retrieval, digital humanities research, educational content recommendation |
| **Marathi Wikipedia Articles Corpus** — Full dump of Marathi Wikipedia articles providing encyclopaedic knowledge coverage across diverse topics in Marathi language. | 90,000+ articles | CC BY-SA 3.0 | Text (Marathi) | [mr.wikipedia.org](https://mr.wikipedia.org/) &#124; [Wikimedia Dumps](https://dumps.wikimedia.org/mrwiki/) | Knowledge base construction, Marathi RAG systems, language model pre-training |
| **Maharashtra Tourism Statistics** — Official tourism data from the Maharashtra Tourism Development Corporation covering domestic and international visitor numbers, tourist destinations, and accommodation statistics. | State-level, annual | Open Government | Tabular (PDF, web) | [Maharashtra Tourism](https://www.maharashtratourism.gov.in/) | Tourism demand forecasting, destination recommendation systems, visitor pattern analysis |
| **Marathi Newspaper Archives** — Historical and contemporary Marathi newspaper collections from major publications (Loksatta, Sakal, Maharashtra Times) available through digital archives. | Multi-year archives | Varies (proprietary/archival) | Text (Marathi) | [Loksatta](https://www.loksatta.com/) &#124; [Sakal](https://www.esakal.com/) | Marathi news corpus building, temporal language modelling, media trend analysis |
| **INTACH Heritage Lists — Maharashtra** — Indian National Trust for Art and Cultural Heritage listings of unprotected heritage sites, buildings, and cultural landscapes in Maharashtra. | Varied listings | Open | Text, Images (web, PDF) | [INTACH](https://www.intach.org/) | Heritage documentation, at-risk site identification, conservation priority modelling |
