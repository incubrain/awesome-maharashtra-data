# Speech & Audio

> Voice and audio datasets for ASR, TTS, speaker identification, and voice-first public services in Marathi.

**Priority**: High | **Target**: 10+ entries

| Dataset | Size | License | Modality | Links | AI Use Case |
|---------|------|---------|----------|-------|-------------|
| **Mozilla Common Voice (Marathi)** — Crowd-sourced read-speech recordings with validated transcriptions for Marathi. | ~30 hrs total, ~21 hrs validated | CC-0 | Speech + Text | [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/mozilla-foundation/common_voice_17_0) / [Website](https://commonvoice.mozilla.org/en/datasets) | ASR, Speaker ID, Pronunciation Modeling |
| **AI4Bharat IndicVoices** — Large-scale multilingual speech corpus with read, extempore, and conversational audio across 22 Indian languages including Marathi. | 7,348 hrs total (22 langs); ~334 hrs/lang median | CC-BY 4.0 | Speech + Text | [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/ai4bharat/IndicVoices) | ASR, Language ID, Speaker Verification |
| **AI4Bharat IndicVoices-R** — ASR-enhanced high-quality TTS corpus for 22 Indian languages; subset of IndicVoices optimized for speech synthesis. | 1,704 hrs total (22 langs); 9-175 hrs/lang | CC-BY 4.0 | Speech + Text (TTS-ready) | [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/ai4bharat/indicvoices_r) | TTS, Voice Cloning, Expressive Speech Synthesis |
| **AI4Bharat Kathbath** — Crowdsourced human-labeled ASR dataset for 12 Indian languages including Marathi, collected via the Karya platform. | 1,684 hrs total (12 langs); ~140 hrs/lang median | CC-0 | Speech + Text | [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/ai4bharat/Kathbath) | ASR, Speech Benchmarking |
| **AI4Bharat Shrutilipi** — Pseudo-labeled ASR corpus mined from All India Radio news bulletins for 12 Indian languages. | 6,400+ hrs total; ~1,020 hrs Marathi | CC-BY 4.0 | Speech + Text | [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/ai4bharat/Shrutilipi) | ASR Pre-training, News Domain ASR |
| **Google FLEURS (Marathi)** — Few-shot speech benchmark derived from FLoRes MT benchmark; read-speech in 102 languages including Marathi (mr_in). | ~10 hrs train; 2,009 sentences total | CC-BY 4.0 | Speech + Text | [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/google/fleurs) | ASR Evaluation, Speech LangID, Multilingual Benchmarking |
| **MUCS 2021 (Marathi)** — Multilingual and code-switching ASR challenge dataset with Marathi speech from diverse speaker groups (college students, rural/urban workers). | ~99 hrs (93.9 hrs train + 5 hrs test); 31 speakers | CC-BY 4.0 | Speech + Text | [OpenSLR-103](https://www.openslr.org/103/) | ASR, Accent-Robust Speech Recognition |
| **OpenSLR-64 (Marathi)** — Crowdsourced high-quality multi-speaker Marathi speech corpus for TTS; female speakers only. | ~3 hrs; 712 MB archive | CC-BY-SA 4.0 | Speech + Text (TTS) | [OpenSLR-64](https://openslr.org/64/) / [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/openslr/openslr) | TTS, Multi-Speaker Voice Synthesis |
| **IIT Madras IndicTTS (Marathi)** — Studio-quality single-speaker TTS corpus with male and female Marathi recordings plus English by native speakers. | ~40 hrs (10 hrs each: male mono, female mono, male English, female English) | Custom academic license (request-based) | Speech + Text (TTS) | [Website](https://www.iitm.ac.in/donlab/indictts/database) | TTS, Speech Synthesis Research |
| **AI4Bharat BhasaAnuvaad (Marathi)** — Largest Indic speech translation dataset with curated, web-mined, and synthetic speech-text pairs for 13 Indian languages. | 44,400 hrs total (13 langs + English) | CC-BY 4.0 | Speech + Text (Translation) | [HuggingFace Collection](https://huggingface.co/collections/ai4bharat/bhasaanuvaad-672b3790b6470eab68b1cb87) | Speech Translation, Multilingual ASR |
| **Microsoft-IITB Marathi Speech Corpus** — Crowdsourced conversational Marathi speech from three user demographics (rural, urban, student). | 109 hrs (93.9 hrs train + 5 hrs test); 36 speakers | Non-commercial research only | Speech + Text | [OpenSLR-103](https://www.openslr.org/103/) / [Microsoft Download](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/details.aspx?id=105292) | ASR, Demographic-Aware Speech Modeling |
| **AI4Bharat Rasa (Marathi)** — Expressive multilingual TTS dataset with neutral and emotional speech (6 Ekman emotions) for 22 Indian languages. | ~20 hrs/speaker; 400 hrs total (22 langs) | CC-BY 4.0 | Speech + Text (Expressive TTS) | [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/ai4bharat/Rasa) | Expressive TTS, Emotion Recognition, Voice Acting |
