# Vision, OCR & Multimodal

> Image, OCR, satellite imagery, and multimodal datasets for document processing, scene understanding, and remote sensing in Maharashtra.

**Priority**: High | **Target**: 8+ entries

| Dataset | Size | License | Modality | Links | AI Use Case |
|---------|------|---------|----------|-------|-------------|
| **CVQA** — Culturally-diverse Multilingual Visual Question Answering benchmark with questions from 30 countries in 31 languages including Marathi. Images and questions are annotated by native speakers familiar with local culture. | ~10K questions across 39 country-language pairs | CC BY-SA (varies per image) | Image + Text | [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/afaji/cvqa) / [Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.05967) | Visual question answering, cultural AI, multimodal reasoning |
| **COCO Captions Marathi** — Marathi translations of MS-COCO image captions, verified by native Marathi speakers for linguistic accuracy and contextual integrity. Useful for training image captioning and cross-lingual retrieval models. | 414K rows of Marathi text | CC BY-NC-ND 4.0 | Text (caption pairs) | [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/Singhchandann/coco-captions_marathi) | Image captioning, semantic search, cross-lingual retrieval |
| **IndicSTR12** — Indic Scene Text Recognition dataset covering 12 major Indian languages including Marathi. Word images collected from natural scenes such as signboards, shop nameplates, railway stations, advertisements, and banners. | 27K+ word images (1K+ per language) | Research use (CVIT/IIIT-H) | Image (scene text) | [Project Page](https://cvit.iiit.ac.in/research/projects/cvit-projects/indicstr) / [Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2403.08007) | Scene text recognition, OCR for Indic scripts |
| **Bharat Scene Text Dataset (BSTD)** — Large-scale scene text dataset for 11 Indian languages plus English, sourced from Wikimedia images of Indian signboards and street scenes. Includes 5,113 Marathi word annotations with polygon bounding boxes. | 6,582 images; 106K+ word instances | Apache-2.0 (images: CC BY-SA 4.0) | Image (scene text) | [GitHub](https://github.com/Bhashini-IITJ/BharatSceneTextDataset) / [Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.23071) | Scene text detection, script identification, end-to-end OCR |
| **ICDAR MLT-2019** — ICDAR 2019 Robust Reading Challenge on Multi-lingual Scene Text Detection and Recognition. Covers 10 languages across 7 scripts including Devanagari, applicable to Marathi scene text. | 20K real images + 277K synthetic images | Research use (competition) | Image (scene text) | [Competition Page](https://rrc.cvc.uab.es/?ch=15) / [Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/1907.00945) | Multilingual scene text detection and recognition |
| **Marathi-OCR-Dataset** — Collection of ~12K Marathi word images with corresponding UTF-8 text labels, sourced from 12 Marathi books across various genres. Images are binarized, thresholded, and resized to 96 dpi for direct neural network input. | ~12K word images | Not specified | Image (printed text) | [GitHub](https://github.com/sayalighodekar/Marathi-OCR-Dataset) | Devanagari OCR, printed text recognition |
| **Devanagari Handwritten Character Dataset** — Handwritten character image database with 46 classes (36 characters + 10 digits) of Devanagari script. Each grayscale image is 32x32 pixels. Applicable to Marathi character recognition since Marathi uses Devanagari. | 92K images (2K per class) | CC BY 4.0 | Image (handwritten characters) | [UCI ML Repository](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/389/devanagari+handwritten+character+dataset) / [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ashokpant/devanagari-character-dataset) | Handwritten character recognition, OCR |
| **IIIT-HW-Dev** — Large-scale Devanagari handwritten word dataset from CVIT, IIIT Hyderabad. Contains word-level images with corrected segmentation. Applicable to Marathi handwriting recognition as Marathi uses Devanagari script. | 95K+ handwritten word images | Research use (CVIT/IIIT-H) | Image (handwritten words) | [Project Page](https://cvit.iiit.ac.in/research/projects/cvit-projects/indic-hw-data) | Handwritten text recognition, document digitization |
| **EuroSAT** — Sentinel-2 satellite image dataset for land use and land cover classification. Contains geo-referenced RGB and multispectral images across 10 classes. Applicable to Maharashtra remote sensing and agricultural monitoring. | 27K images (64x64 px), 10 classes | MIT | Satellite imagery (RGB + multispectral) | [GitHub](https://github.com/phelber/EuroSAT) / [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/blanchon/EuroSAT_RGB) | Land cover classification, remote sensing, agricultural monitoring |
| **NWPU-RESISC45** — Large-scale remote sensing image scene classification benchmark with 45 scene classes extracted from Google Earth covering 100+ countries. Applicable to Maharashtra urban planning, land use mapping, and infrastructure analysis. | 31.5K images (256x256 px), 45 classes | Research use | Satellite imagery (RGB) | [HuggingFace](https://huggingface.co/datasets/timm/resisc45) / [TensorFlow](https://www.tensorflow.org/datasets/catalog/resisc45) | Remote sensing scene classification, urban planning |
