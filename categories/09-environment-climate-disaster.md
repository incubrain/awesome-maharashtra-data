# Environment, Climate & Disaster

> Climate data, air quality, flood monitoring, forest cover, and disaster resilience datasets for Maharashtra environmental AI and early warning systems.

**Priority**: Medium | **Target**: 10+ entries

| Dataset | Size | License | Modality | Links | AI Use Case |
|---------|------|---------|----------|-------|-------------|
| **CPCB Air Quality Index — Maharashtra Stations** — Continuous ambient air quality monitoring (PM2.5, PM10, NO2, SO2, O3, CO) from CAAQMS stations across Maharashtra cities including Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur, Aurangabad. | Hourly, 20+ stations | Open Government | Tabular (CSV, API) | [CPCB CCR](https://airquality.cpcb.gov.in/ccr/) &#124; [AQI Report](https://cpcb.nic.in/aqi_report.php) &#124; [CPCB ENVIS](https://cpcbenvis.nic.in/air_quality_data.html) | AQI forecasting, pollution source attribution, health impact modelling |
| **CWC Flood Forecasting — Maharashtra River Gauges** — Real-time and historical water level and discharge data from river gauging stations across Maharashtra's major river basins (Krishna, Godavari, Tapi). | Hourly (monsoon), 340 stations nationally | Open Government | Tabular (web portal) | [Flood Forecast](https://ffs.india-water.gov.in/) &#124; [CWC Portal](https://cwc.gov.in/flood-forecasting-hydrological-observation) | Flood forecasting, river discharge prediction, dam inflow modelling |
| **IMD Climate Data — Maharashtra Stations** — Historical climate normals, rainfall, and temperature data from India Meteorological Department stations across Maharashtra. | Multi-decadal, station-level | Open (data request) | Tabular (PDF, web portal) | [IMD Mausam](https://mausam.imd.gov.in/) &#124; [Climate Services](https://mausam.imd.gov.in/responsive/climate_services.php) &#124; [Rainfall Statistics](https://mausam.imd.gov.in/responsive/rainfall_statistics.php?PAGE=1) | Climate trend analysis, rainfall prediction, agricultural weather advisory |

| **India-WRIS / NRSC Flood Inundation Maps** — Satellite-based near real-time flood inundation extent maps for Maharashtra during monsoon events from ISRO/NRSC. | Event-based, spatial | Open Government | Geospatial (raster, web maps) | [NRSC Flood Monitoring](https://ndrf.nrsc.gov.in/hydrological_flood.php?lang=en) &#124; [Bhuvan Disaster](https://bhuvan-app1.nrsc.gov.in/disaster/disaster.php) | Flood extent estimation, damage assessment, evacuation planning |
| **ERA5 Reanalysis (ECMWF) — Maharashtra** — Hourly global climate reanalysis data (temperature, precipitation, wind, humidity) at 0.25 degree resolution covering Maharashtra from 1940 to present. | Hourly, 0.25deg grid, 1940–present | Open (Copernicus License) | Gridded (NetCDF, GRIB) | [Copernicus CDS](https://cds.climate.copernicus.eu/datasets/reanalysis-era5-single-levels?tab=download) &#124; [Google Earth Engine](https://developers.google.com/earth-engine/datasets/catalog/ECMWF_ERA5_LAND_HOURLY) | Climate modelling, historical weather reconstruction, crop-weather modelling |

| **NOAA Climate Data Online — India Stations** — Historical weather observations (temperature, precipitation, wind) from NOAA's global surface summary of day stations in Maharashtra. | Daily, multi-decadal | Open (US Public Domain) | Tabular (CSV) | [NOAA CDO](https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/cdo-web/) | Climate trend analysis, station-level weather modelling, quality control benchmarking |
| **Maharashtra SDMA Disaster Data** — State Disaster Management Authority data on hazard vulnerability, disaster events, and emergency response covering floods, cyclones, earthquakes in Maharashtra. | State-level, event-based | Open Government | Tabular (web, PDF) | [SDMA Maharashtra](https://sdma.maharashtra.gov.in/en/hazard-vulnerability-risk-information/) | Disaster preparedness modelling, risk-informed urban planning, emergency response optimisation |
| **India Flood Atlas** — Gridded simulations of annual maximum flood extent across India (including Maharashtra) from 1901–2020 based on hydrological modelling. | 1901–2020, gridded | Open | Geospatial (web maps) | [India Flood Atlas](https://indiafloodatlas.in/) | Long-term flood risk assessment, climate change impact analysis, land-use planning |
